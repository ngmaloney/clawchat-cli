## Fork Guidelines

Forks are welcome under the terms of the GNU General Public License v3.0.
If you fork ClawChat, you must:

- Clearly state your project is a fork of ClawChat
- Link back to the official repository
- Not use "ClawChat" as the primary name of your fork
- Not imply your fork is the official or original project
- Retain all copyright, license, and attribution notices as required by GPL v3

## Contributor Protections

Contributors to the official ClawChat repositories are protected under GPL v3:

- **Copyleft** — derivative works must remain open source under GPL v3.
- **No trademark transfer** — contributing code does not transfer any

## Reporting Infringement

If you believe someone is infringing ClawChat trademarks:

2. Include the URL of the infringing content
3. Describe how it violates this policy

For serious or commercial infringement, contact The Gnar Company Inc.
directly through the repository.
